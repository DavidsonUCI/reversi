Rules

Vampires vs Zombies is a strategy board game for two players, played on an 8Ã—8 uncheckered board. There are sixty-four identical game pieces called discs which are Vampires on one side and Zombies on the other.

Players take turns placing disks on the board with their assigned color facing up.

During a play, any discs of the opponent's side that are in a straight line and bounded by the disk just placed and another disk of the current player's side are turned over to the current player's side. The object of the game is to have the majority of discs turned to display your side when the last playable empty square is filled.
